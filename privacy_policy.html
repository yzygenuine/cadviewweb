1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how CAD FastView King (the “App”) handles information when you use the App on iOS devices.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information through the App.

The App works primarily with files you choose to open (such as CAD drawings and PDFs). These files are processed on your device.

  • Files you import/open: Drawing files and documents you select remain on your device unless you choose to share or sync them.
  • iCloud: If you use iCloud features, Apple iCloud may store your files according to your iCloud settings.
  • In‑App Purchases: Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment information (such as card details).

3. How We Use Information

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not use your personal information for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

We use on-device data (such as the files you open) only to provide core app features, including viewing, exporting to PDF, printing, and syncing when enabled.

4. Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties because we do not collect it.

You may choose to share files using iOS system features (e.g., Share Sheet, printing, AirDrop). Any sharing is initiated by you and governed by the recipient service/provider.

5. Retention

We do not retain personal information on our servers.

Files and exports are stored on your device and/or in your iCloud storage (if enabled) until you delete them.

6. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards within the App to protect files processed on-device. No method of storage is 100% secure, so you should also protect your device with a passcode and keep iOS updated.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, delete, or correct personal information. Because we do not collect personal information, requests generally relate to files stored on your device or in iCloud, which you can manage through iOS and iCloud settings.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
